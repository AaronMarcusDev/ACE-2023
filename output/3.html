England trip TTO VWO 3

The trip to England with year 3 was

one of the most fun and unique

experiences I have had. I have gotten

to try the English dishes, spoken to

English people, been to English theatres

and much more. I found that the English

culture resembled the Dutch culture a

lot, but there were some differences.

The English people were very friendly

and helpful.
