Lithuania exchange

In year 3, students from Lithuania came

to our school. We had to work together

in groups to create a video about how

our school could be more climate

friendly. I had a lot of fun

working with the exchange students

to create our final product. It was

a fun and educational experience

I will not forget.



Lizdeika Gymnasia, the school we did
