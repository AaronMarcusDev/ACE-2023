Holiday in Denmark

In the previous summer break, I went

to Denmark with my Parents and my

little sister. We were staying in a

camping on the island 'island'. We

have visited lots of beautiful

environments, one that will remember

Is the 'forest' forest. We have also

visited Copenhaven (Kobenhavn) and

What I noticed was how many different

cultures can be found on this city.

Poeple from all kinds of different

backgrounds were running stores in

the streets of Kobenhavn, it was

really fun to see all of these cultures

living together in one big city.

Everyone in Denmark also spoke perfect

English! Great holiday destination.
